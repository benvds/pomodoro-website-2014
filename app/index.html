Why does this matter?

The right experience gives you a feeling of control, piece and confidence. It is founded on the understanding of and anticipations the users's needs while also meeting business requirements.

Removing confusion and frustration results in happier users, more commitment and allows for higher conversion and a more sustainable relationship. This is key in online success.

This doesn't mean more work, but work smarter. It starts with a baseline study and results in a well thought out strategy and design which enable you to focus on only the most effective parts of the product, making sure development time is spent on things that truly matter.
